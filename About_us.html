Story of our App

Despite the abundance environmentally themed games in the market, very few of them are both fun and engaging. Among that number, only a handful of them are meant to serve as a means to educate the player about the various environmental issues. Thus, we wanted to create an environmentally themed game that is realistic, fun, challenging, and somewhat educational.

And that's how EcoLibria was born.
